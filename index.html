      📱 Added to Home Screen?
      When opening from the Home Screen icon, the QR login link won't auto-configure the app.

      To restore credentials:
      1. Open Safari and scan the team guide QR code — this saves credentials
      2. Then switch back to the Home Screen app — it shares the same login

      Or enter credentials manually in WooCommerce Connection above.
      How to use
      1. Connect with WooCommerce credentials above — guest list loads automatically
      2. Scan tab — point camera at QR code on ticket
      3. Green ✓ = admitted · Red ✗ = rejected · Yellow ⚠ = already in or too early
      4. Guests tab — search by name, tap to expand, admit individually or all at once
      5. Evening tickets — valid from 5:00 PM only
      6. Ticketmaster barcodes — app flags for visual verify, then you admit/reject manually
      7. Scanner and guest list stay in sync automatically every 3 seconds
      Ticketmaster guests: import into WooCommerce as £0 completed orders before the event. Add "Ticketmaster" to the product name and the app will flag them automatically when their name is searched.

      VIP: detected when product name contains "VIP".
      Comps: any completed order with a £0 total.
